# Key Assumptions and Timeline

## 1. Timeline
- **Start Date**: 1 July 2024
- **End Date**: 30 June 2029 (5 Years)
- **Structure**: 60 monthly periods for calculations, with annual summary outputs.

## 3. Financial Assumptions
- **Corporate Tax**: 30% (Australia).
- **GST**: 10% (Calculated on Net Revenue and most OpEx).
- **Inflation**: 3.0% annually.
- **DSO (Accounts Receivable)**: 30 days.
- **DPO (Accounts Payable)**: 45 days.
- **Inventory Days**: 60 days (Malt/Hops/Finished Goods).

## 4. Operational Assumptions
- **Capacity**: 75,000 HL initially.
- **Expansion Trigger**: $4,500,000 CAPEX when utilization > 85%.
- **Excise Rate (Feb 2024)**:
    - Packaged: $60.22 / Litre of Alcohol (LAL)
    - Draught: $40.50 / LAL
    - *Note: First 1.15% ABV is exempt.*

## 5. Scenario Parameters
- **Scenario 1 (Base)**: Standard growth and stable costs.
- **Scenario 2 (Raw Material Spike)**: +25% Malt and Hops prices from Month 13.
- **Scenario 3 (Market Shift)**: Off-trade volume grows 2x faster than On-trade (margin compression).
